3) A written abstract on the project. Max 100 words:
You are God! However your dysfunctional cousin is also a God and apparently gaining new religious followers too swiftly for your liking. Use taboos to burn your colleague's followers while keeping your own minions out of temptation and harms way. 
Players shuffle their own people about simultaneously trying to avoid compromising social combinations that could be used against them by a wicked god. After 5-20 seconds of frantic movement one predestinated player will select a taboo. The taboo will hit all people gathering in a specific combination of males and females. Repeat till one side is dead.
